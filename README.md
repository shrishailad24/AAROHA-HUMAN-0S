# 🌍 AAROHA – Human Operating System

> **One AI. Every Life Decision.**

AAROHA is an AI-powered Human Operating System designed to help people make smarter decisions throughout every stage of life. Instead of acting as a traditional chatbot, AAROHA combines information from multiple life domains—such as finance, education, career, health, and civic services—to provide personalized, explainable, and actionable recommendations while keeping users in complete control of their data.

---

## 🚀 Vision

Today's applications solve individual problems.

- 📍 Google Maps → Navigation
- 💳 PhonePe → Payments
- 💼 LinkedIn → Careers
- 📝 Notion → Notes
- ☁ Google Drive → Storage

**AAROHA's vision is to become the AI layer that connects every aspect of a person's life and helps users make better decisions through intelligent reasoning.**

---

# 🎯 Why AAROHA?

Most AI assistants answer one question at a time.

AAROHA goes beyond answering questions.

It understands a user's financial status, education, career goals, health information, and personal knowledge to provide recommendations that consider the complete picture.

### Example

**User asks**

> Should I buy a bike?

Instead of simply answering "Yes" or "No", AAROHA analyzes:

- Monthly Income
- Savings
- Expenses
- Education Costs
- Loan Eligibility
- Financial Goals
- Daily Travel Distance

Then provides an explainable recommendation based on the user's situation.

# 🧠 Central AI Decision Engine

The Central AI Decision Engine is the heart of AAROHA.

Unlike traditional AI assistants, it combines information from all Brains to provide intelligent, explainable, and context-aware recommendations.

### Example 1

**User**

> Am I placement ready?

The AI analyzes:

- Resume
- Skills
- GitHub
- Projects
- Certifications
- Interview Performance
- Internship Experience

Then generates:

- Placement Readiness Score
- Missing Skills
- Recommended Learning Path
- Target Companies

---

### Example 2

**User**

> What should I learn this month?

The AI analyzes:

- Current Skills
- Career Goals
- Placement Timeline
- Industry Demand
- Previous Learning Progress

Then creates a personalized roadmap.

---

### Example 3

**User**

> Can I afford a new laptop?

The AI combines:

- Income
- Savings
- Monthly Expenses
- Loan Eligibility
- Upcoming Education Costs

Then provides a detailed financial recommendation.
